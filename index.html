Portfolio Résumé

Welcome to My Webspace!

I'm not being totally serious—this is a tongue-in-cheek homage to the late 90s, when people often wrote these greetings on their personal sites. It's also when I first started writing code.

I taught myself (table-based) HTML with the help of a site called HTML Goodies, and hand-coded everything in Notepad, because I'm pretty sure that's all there was.

I consider myself fortunate to have been a part of this profession since the very beginning. Over twenty years later, I still have a love of building with code (and a whole lot of perspective). Here's to another twenty!
